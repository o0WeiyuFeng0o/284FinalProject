We’ll be trying to make a physically accurate simulation of how Covid particles spread and ideally do it in real time so that users can interact with it by changing conditions (masks, wind, humidity, ventilation). This will require an efficient particle simulation algorithm, and especially so if we make it in 3D instead of 2D. Depending on how much time we have left, we’ll then add an additional layer of complexity by incorporating human crowds (2D boids) on top of the cough simulation. Although it may not be 100% accurate to real life, our final deliverable could be used as a tool to study/present the covid spread in different settings. The biggest challenge will likely be getting this all to run in real time at a reasonable frame rate.
