CS284A Final Project

Richard Wang, Weiyu Feng, Jordan Wong, Yi Chen Chen

Overview

With the relevancy and potency of SARS-CoV-2 or Covid19 in recent memory, scientists have been working hard to learn more about how this deadly pathogen is transmitted. Our project is working to create a simulation of how Covid particles are physically spread through the air. We will then potentially add a human crowd / boids simulation on top of that to see how different conditions in people's “pathing” and social distancing affect Covid spread.
